Answer: [Git lets you track every change you make to your code, so you can always go back to earlier versions if you mess up. Multiple people can

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: [Merging combines two branches together and keeps the full history, while rebasing moves your changes on
 top of another branch to make the history look cleaner and linear.]
